Generic interpolation constraints example 1

In this case, a generic interpolation constraint was created to make the thermal plants generation T1 and T2, follow the segments described in the interpolation constraint:

$$ \begin{vmatrix} T_1 \\ T_2 \end{vmatrix} = \lambda_1 \cdot \begin{vmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{vmatrix} + \lambda_2 \cdot \begin{vmatrix} 4 \\ 1 \end{vmatrix} + \lambda_3 \cdot \begin{vmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \end{vmatrix} + \lambda_4 \cdot \begin{vmatrix} 16 \\ 8 \end{vmatrix} + \lambda_5 \cdot \begin{vmatrix} 25 \\ 15 \end{vmatrix} + \begin{vmatrix} \alpha_1 \\ \alpha_1 \end{vmatrix} $$
$$ \lambda_k <= y_{k-1} + y_k, \forall k \subset \left[1,5\right] $$
$$ \begin{align} \sum_{k=1}^{5} \lambda_k = 1 & \qquad \sum_{k=0}^{5} y_k = 1 \end{align} $$
$$ \begin{align} y_0 = 0 & \qquad y_5 = 0 \end{align} $$
$$ \begin{align} y = \lbrace 0,1 \rbrace ^6 & \qquad \lambda = \left[0,1\right]^5 \end{align} $$
